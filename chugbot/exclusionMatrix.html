De-Duplication Matrix

This page lets you tell the system that certain chugim should not be assigned to the same camper in the same block as other chugim.

To disallow a chug combination, find the first chug on the left side, and match it to the column headed by the second chug. For example, to prevent the system from assigning Cooking and Outdoor Cooking to the same camper in July 1, find the row with "Cooking" on the left, then locate the column with "Outdoor Cooking" at the top, and click the checkbox at the intersection of that row/column.

Note that by default, chugim are de-duplicated with themselves.

When you are done, click "Save Changes" at the bottom of this page.
