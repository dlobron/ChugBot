EDAH_TERM_COMBO: EDAH

Time BLOCK_TERM: BLOCK

This page displays suggested CHUG assignments for one EDAH_TERM: in a time BLOCK_TERM. Assignments are highlighted according to the camper's preference: first, second, third, fourth or worse. Campers with no preferences for a CHUG group are highlighted in purple.

  • To change a camper's assignment, drag the camper's bubble from one activity to another. When you are ready to save your manual changes, click Save. To see a camper's preferred CHUGIM, hover your mouse over the bubble for that camper.
  • To re-run the assignment algorithm, click Reassign. This will reassign campers automatically.
  • If a camper signed up late, or changed registration, they might not have an assignment yet. Such campers will appear in a box labeled Not Assigned Yet. To assign these campers, you can drag them to a CHUG and click Save, or click the Reassign button.
  • NOTE: campers who have not submitted preferences are NOT automatically assigned; they are highlighted in purple and appear in the Not Assigned Yet box.
  • To exit this page and return to the staff home page, click Done.
  • To design custom schedules with each camper's saved CHUG assignments, click Design Schedules.
